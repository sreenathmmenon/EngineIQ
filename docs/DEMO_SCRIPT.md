## STAGE DIRECTIONS & DELIVERY NOTES

### Pacing
- **Total: 5 minutes** (with demo execution time)
- Opening: 45 seconds
- Demo 1: 60 seconds
- Demo 2: 45 seconds

### Emotional Arc
1. **Opening:** Empathy (frustration, helplessness)
2. **Demo 1-4:** Hope and empowerment (solutions working)
3. **Closing:** Triumph and conviction (lives changed)

### Voice Modulation
- **Slow down** for emotional moments (Priya at 2am, Diego invisible)
- **Speed up slightly** for technical demonstrations (builds excitement)
- **Pause** before key impact statements (let them sink in)
- **Volume up** for conviction statements (equity, empowerment)

**Q: "What happens if Gemini or Claude goes down?"**
